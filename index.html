What Canopy does

The whole garden
in one calm app.

Schedules that adapt

Watering, misting, feeding, repotting — each on its own cadence, tracked with streaks so you can see your care over time.

Photos of every plant

Snap from your camera roll or shoot in-app. Each plant gets its own profile — name, room, notes, the works.

Extensive plant database

Hundreds of common houseplants built in with sensible default schedules. Don't see yours? Add it — Canopy remembers.

Gentle daily reminders

One notification a day, at the time you choose. No streak guilt, no manipulative nudges — just what's due.

Light, dark, and yours

Auto-matches your system theme. Seven accent colors to choose from. Looks great either way.

Yours, on your phone

Everything stays on your device. No account, no cloud, no analytics. Your plants are nobody's business but yours.
